About

I’m an editor and photographer chasing stories worth remembering. From editing over sixty wedding films to directing short films and capturing distant galaxies through astrophotography, I’m always searching for moments that feel real, cinematic, and unforgettable.

Currently a Film graduate from London South Bank University, I’m fluent across the full production pipeline — from on-set assistance and production planning through to editing, colour, conform, and final delivery. A film I worked on during my studies, Apple Pie, earned official selection at film festivals including New York Lift-Off, Los Angeles Lift-Off, and Global Lift-Off in 2025.

I believe the edit is where a film truly finds itself — where ideas are discovered, broken apart, and rebuilt through rhythm and emotion. Every cut carries intention. With a foundation in post-production and the perspective of a filmmaker, I approach storytelling with both technical discipline and a detail-oriented creative eye.

I’m actively seeking collaborations and opportunities within post-production and filmmaking to continue evolving my craft, expand my creative network, and work toward directing my own feature film in the future.
